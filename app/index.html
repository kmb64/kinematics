About Us

Kinematics Health and Performance Clinic provides a complete approach to the diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation of muscular and skeletal issues. Our team has over 30 years of experience in musculoskeletal assessments in Podiatry and Physiotherapy, with a demonstrated ability to meet the needs of elite athletes and the general public alike.

Kinematics specialises in the assessment of functional and sport specific biomechanics (movement patterns) of the whole body in order to identify areas of imbalance. If not addressed, these imbalances and poor mechanics often lead to secondary compensations in the body and cascade on to cause other injuries.

This holistic, whole body approach enables the effective treatment of not only the presenting issue but also the underlying primary cause, reducing the risk of the persistence and re-occurrence of injuries.

Meet Our Team

Laupepa (Pep) Karalus

Musculoskeletal Biomechanist

Founder of Kinematics

Pep has been working as a musculoskeletal sports clinician for the past 7 years. Pep specialises in the assessment of whole body biomechanics with a special interest in lower limb musculoskeletal injuries.

Pep started out his career studying Physiotherapy before transitioning on to do Podiatry in order to gain a more extensive understanding of the ground reaction forces of the foot and the result of these forces on the rest of the body. After completing Podiatry, Pep furthered his biomechanical knowledge by completing multiple running and performance courses.

Throughout his career, Pep has worked for provincial, national and international sporting teams and was a provider for the New Zealand Rugby Union for 5 years. Pep has also worked closely with players from Otago ITM and Super 15 Rugby teams as well as representative athletics, basketball, cricket and netball players.

Pep assesses the biomechanics and alignment of the body as a whole and does not just treat in area isolation. Treatment techniques include; foot, ankle and knee mobilisations, dry needling, remedial massage, Prolotherapy and exercise rehabilitation. Pep works closely alongside Ken Craig, the Vice President of the International Society of Medical Shockwave Treatment and founder of Isokinetics and Shockwave Health and Performance Clinic.

Pep is a national distributor of KenesioLogic taping and in the near future looks forward to running biomechanics courses incorporating education on the correct application of this groundbreaking product.
